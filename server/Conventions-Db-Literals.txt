
Status :-

The status of Tables suggests various things - 

1 - Active not sold ready to be sold out,

0 - In-active sold out or purchased by a customer

-1 - Cannot be sold due to some discripancy in paper work etc. any possible reason.

Rating :-

Rating is an integer that is used for representing the worth or trendiness of the product 
in the database, its directly proportional to the value assigned with the product.

User Type - 

1 - Normal User or Individual

2 - Dealer

3 - Company Dealer
